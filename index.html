Brazzavill

The Republic of Congo, also known as Congo-Brazzaville, is a country located in Central Africa. It is bordered by Gabon to the west, Cameroon and the Central African Republic to the north, the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the east and south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the southwest. Its capital is Brazzaville, situated along the Congo River across from Kinshasa, the capital of the DRC. The country has a population of approximately 5.8 million people. Life in the Republic of Congo is a blend of traditional and modern lifestyles, with many people living in urban centers while others maintain rural, agricultural lives. French is the official language, and the culture is rich with music, dance, and diverse ethnic traditions.
